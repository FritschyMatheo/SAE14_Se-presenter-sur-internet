What is Street Lifting?

Streetlifting is based on the Set and Rep exercises, with the exception of push-ups, but with the addition of the heaviest possible load. This is one of the most established categories, with competitions and more official rules. The aim in a competition is to lift as much weight as possible, adding up the maximum repetition for each movement. So we have the following movements:

  • Weighted pullups
  • Weighted dips
  • Weighted Muscle ups
  • Squats

Participants weight themselves down with a weight belt connected to weights by a chain, and are assisted by a box to get into the starting position for the movement, supervised by referees. The referees check the execution of the movement to validate or invalidate it.
