What is dynamic?

Dynamics may not immediately spring to mind when you think of callisthenics or Street Workout, but it's part of it too. Unlike static, which involves standing figures, dynamic is all about doing the most impressive and complex movements around a fairly long pull-up bar. This is a rather frightening category, since it's easier to injure yourself, and the movements are quickly very impressive. For example, there's the "360", which consists of creating momentum and then going backwards, letting go of the bar, then turning 360° and catching the bar again. Not all figures require you to let go of the bar, but there are quite a few. You can compare it to the high bar in gymnastics, except that it's not made of wood and isn't always long. You have to adapt to the environment.
