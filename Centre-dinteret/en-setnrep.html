What is Set and Reps ?

Set and Reps, also known as "Set n Reps", is a better-known facet of callisthenics, or at least its exercises. The exercises in this branch are :

  • Pushups
  • Dips
  • Pullups
  • Squats
  • (Muscle ups)

The goal of the Set and Reps is to perform the cleanest repetition possible and to do either as many as possible for endurance, or as many as possible in the shortest possible time, which works on explosiveness. As you may have noticed, leg exercises are not very present in callisthenics, which is one of the sport's weak points. The name comes from the words "Sets" and "reps".
This category is in fact the basis of callisthenics, and as a beginner it is often advisable to start here to create a good base before moving on to ballasting or more complicated skills.

There are also variants of these exercices that could also be classified in this category, such as the one-arm or one-leg variants of these movements: The pistol squat, single-arm pull-up or single-arm pump. You can also change the support or the grip, such as dips on a straight bar, or pull-ups in a neutral grip or on rings. The muscle-up has been bracketed above, as it's already a more advanced and less widespread movement, but some practitioners still include it in the sport. In competition, execution is supervised by referees to prevent cheating.
