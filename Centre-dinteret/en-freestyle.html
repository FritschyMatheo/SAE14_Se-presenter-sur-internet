What is freestyle?

Freestyle is simply the combination of static and dynamic. This is the most conventional format used for "Battles". Several contestants compete in what are known as "combos" of static and dynamic figures. Simply put, this is either a sequence of static figures lasting at least 3s each without touching the ground, or a combination of static and dynamic figures, or sometimes dynamic skills only. In general, athletes specialize in strength or dynamics, but each one masters at least a few transition movements and strength (static) skills.
In competitions, candidates receive points awarded by judges according to criteria of difficulty, originality and risk. In France, we have Sangohan Otz, the 2018 world champion and one of the best freestyle athletes in the world.
